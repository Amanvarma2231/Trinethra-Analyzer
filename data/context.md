# Trinethra - Domain Context

## Fellow Model

DeepThought Fellows are early-career professionals (1-5 years experience) placed full-time in manufacturing MSMEs for 3-6 months. They work on:
- Operations structuring
- Process documentation
- Daily accountability systems
- Execution gap diagnosis

## Supervisor Call Protocol

Psychology interns conduct monthly 15-minute calls with client supervisors. The calls are semi-structured:
1. Open-ended: "How is the Fellow doing?"
2. Probe for specifics: "Can you give me an example?"
3. Cover all dimensions indirectly
4. Avoid leading questions
5. End with: "What score would you give out of 10?"

## KPI Definitions

1. **OTD (On-Time Delivery)**: % of orders delivered on or before committed date
2. **Quality**: Product defect rate, rework %, customer returns
3. **Productivity**: Output per labor hour or machine hour
4. **Cost Reduction**: Measurable decrease in operational costs
5. **Employee Retention**: Staff turnover rate improvement
6. **Capacity Utilization**: % of available machine/labor capacity used
7. **Lead Time**: Time from order to delivery
8. **Customer Satisfaction**: NPS, repeat orders, complaint frequency

## Supervisor Bias Patterns

Psychology interns are trained to watch for:
- **Recency bias**: Supervisor focuses on last 2 weeks
- **Halo effect**: One good trait colors everything positive
- **Leniency bias**: Giving high scores to avoid conflict
- **Strictness bias**: "No one gets above 7"
- **Task vs. System focus**: Supervisors often notice task completion but miss systems building
